# Privacy Policy — Mobile Master Mechanic

**Last updated:** April 30, 2026

Angie's Auto Supplies operates the **Mobile Master Mechanic** mobile application, including all related information, content, features, AI diagnostic tools, and services, in order to provide you, the user, with an AI-assisted vehicle diagnosis and service-tracking experience (the "App"). Mobile Master Mechanic is powered by Supabase (database, authentication, file storage) and Anthropic (the AI engine that produces diagnoses), which enable us to provide the App to you. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you download, register for, or use the App or otherwise communicate with us in connection with it.

This Privacy Policy applies to the **App only**. For information about how we handle personal information in connection with our website and online store at **carlotsupplies.com**, please see our [website Privacy Policy](https://carlotsupplies.com/policies/privacy-policy). If you use both the App and our website, both policies apply to you.

If there is a conflict between our Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy, this Privacy Policy controls with respect to the collection, processing, and disclosure of your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using and accessing the App, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy and understand the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## Personal Information We Collect or Process

When we use the term "personal information," we are referring to information that identifies or can reasonably be linked to you. Personal information does not include information that is collected anonymously or that has been de-identified, so that it cannot identify or be reasonably linked to you.

We may collect or process the following categories of personal information, depending on how you interact with the App, where you live, and as permitted or required by applicable law:

- **Contact details** including your name (or display name) and email address.
- **Account information** including your username, password (stored as a one-way hash; we never see your password in plain text), preferences, and settings.
- **Vehicle information** including the year, make, model, trim, mileage, transmission type, and any optional photos or notes you add to vehicle profiles in your in-app garage.
- **Diagnosis content** including the questions, symptoms, descriptions, and photos you submit during a diagnosis session, along with the AI-generated diagnoses, work orders, and parts recommendations the App produces in response.
- **Subscription information** including subscription product ID, purchase token, and entitlement state. Payment card numbers, bank account numbers, and other financial details are processed by **Google Play Billing** (Android) or the **Apple App Store** (iOS) and are not visible to us.
- **Communications with us** including the information you include in communications with us, for example, when sending a customer support inquiry.
- **Device information** including device model, operating system version, app version, language preference, and an anonymous device identifier used for crash reporting and subscription state.
- **Usage information** including information regarding your interaction with the App, including which screens you view, which features you use, and crash and performance diagnostics.

The App requests the following device permissions on demand:

- **Camera** — used only when you choose to photograph a vehicle issue for diagnosis.
- **Photo library** — used only when you choose to attach an existing photo to a vehicle or diagnosis.

The App does **not** access your location, contacts, microphone, calendar, or files outside what you explicitly choose to attach.

Depending on how you interact with us or which features of the App you use, we may use personal information for the following purposes:

- **Provide, Tailor, and Improve the App.** We use your personal information to provide you with the App's functionality, including authenticating your account, generating AI-assisted diagnoses based on your vehicle and symptom information, saving your diagnosis history and garage records, processing your subscription, sending notifications related to your account, and creating a personalized experience.

## How We Disclose Personal Information

In certain circumstances, we may disclose your personal information to third parties for legitimate purposes subject to this Privacy Policy. Such circumstances may include:

- **With service providers who perform services on our behalf**, including:
  - **Supabase** — database, authentication, and file storage. Hosted in the United States.
  - **Anthropic (Claude)** — the AI engine that processes diagnosis questions and generates work orders. We send your symptom descriptions and vehicle context, but we do **not** send your name, email, or other identifying information to Anthropic.
  - **Google Play Billing / Apple App Store** — subscription processing.
  - **RevenueCat** *(if and when enabled)* — subscription state management. Receives only an anonymous user ID and purchase token.
  - **Expo Application Services** — app build infrastructure, anonymized crash reporting, and over-the-air updates.
- **When you direct, request, or otherwise consent** to our disclosure of certain information to a third party.
- **With our affiliates or otherwise within our corporate group**, including any future related entities operating under Angie's Auto Supplies.
- **In connection with a business transaction** such as a merger or bankruptcy, **to comply with any applicable legal obligations** (including to respond to subpoenas, search warrants, and similar requests), **to enforce** any applicable terms of service or policies, and **to protect or defend** the App, our rights, and the rights of our users or others.

Each service provider listed above acts as a data processor on our behalf and is bound by its own privacy policy and data protection agreement.

## Data Storage, Security, and Retention

- Data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the United States.
- All connections between the App and our servers use HTTPS (TLS encryption).
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.
- Account, vehicle, and diagnosis data are retained for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, data is permanently removed within **30 days**, except where retention is required by law (for example, billing records).
- Anonymized crash logs are retained for 90 days.

No system is perfectly secure. While we work hard to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
