# Data Deletion Request

**Last updated:** April 30, 2026

This page explains how to request that Angie's Auto Supplies delete your account and associated personal data, whether you use our **online store at carlotsupplies.com**, our **Mobile Master Mechanic** mobile app, or both.

We honor data deletion requests for all users, regardless of where you live or which laws apply to you.

---

## Option 1: Delete data inside the Mobile Master Mechanic app

The fastest way to erase your app data is from inside the app itself:

1. Open Mobile Master Mechanic on your phone.
2. Tap the **Settings** tab (gear icon, bottom right).
3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen.
4. Tap **Reset App / Delete All My Data**.
5. Confirm both prompts.

This **immediately** erases:

- All vehicles in your garage (year/make/model, mileage, photos, notes)
- All diagnosis history and AI conversations
- All work orders and service records
- All earned points and rewards

The reset is permanent. There is no undo. After reset, the app behaves as if it were freshly installed.

> **Note:** A reset clears all *content* but does not delete your account record itself (email, login). To also delete your account, follow Option 3 below.

---

## Option 2: Delete your carlotsupplies.com store account

To request deletion of your online store account at carlotsupplies.com (order history, addresses, billing info), email us using the address below. Include:

- The email address associated with your store account
- Subject line: **Delete my carlotsupplies.com account**

We will confirm receipt within 5 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (for example, billing and tax records, which we keep for the period required by federal and Oregon state law).

---

## Option 3: Delete everything (app + website + all records)

If you want a full erasure across both products, email us using the address below. Include:

- The email address(es) associated with your account(s)
- Subject line: **Delete all my data — full erasure**

We will:

1. Confirm receipt within **5 business days**.
2. Verify your identity (so we don't delete the wrong person's data).
3. Permanently delete the following within **30 days**:
   - Mobile Master Mechanic account, vehicles, diagnoses, photos, points, rewards
   - carlotsupplies.com store account, addresses, profile data
   - Stored marketing preferences and email subscriptions

We retain the following only when required by law and only for the legally required period:

- Billing records and invoices (for tax and accounting compliance)
- Records of fraud or security incidents (if applicable)
- Anything subject to an active legal hold

We do **not** retain or sell your personal information for any other purpose after deletion is complete.

---

## What is **not** deleted by these requests

- Records held by third parties we do not control. Examples:
  - Apple App Store or Google Play purchase history (request deletion via Apple/Google)
  - Payment processors (Shopify Payments, Stripe — billing data subject to their retention policies)
  - Anonymous, aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked back to you
- Public reviews or ratings you may have left on Google Play or third-party sites (request these directly with the platform)
