## Full description (≤ 4,000 characters, currently 2,841)

```
MOBILE MASTER MECHANIC — Your AI Master Tech, Anywhere

Meet Hank. He's a thirty-year master mechanic in your pocket, and he's ready to diagnose what's wrong with your car, build you a step-by-step work order, and tell you exactly what parts to buy.

No more guessing. No more shop visits just to hear "we'll have to take a look." Describe the symptom, answer Hank's follow-up questions, and get a real diagnosis — with confidence ratings, severity, and a complete plan.

WHAT HANK CAN DO

• Diagnose check-engine lights, weird noises, fluid leaks, performance issues, electrical gremlins, and just about anything else that goes wrong with a vehicle.
• Build complete work orders with parts lists, OEM part numbers, estimated costs, and step-by-step repair instructions you can actually follow.
• Track every vehicle in your virtual garage — year, make, model, mileage, photos, service history, and notes.
• Save every diagnosis so you can reference it later, share it with a shop, or print a PDF.
• Recommend the right shop supplies for the job, sourced from a real auto-supply dealer (Angie's Auto Supplies, in business since the 1960s).

WHO THIS APP IS FOR

• Weekend warriors fixing their own car.
• Independent shop owners getting a second opinion before quoting a customer.
• Used-car buyers checking out a vehicle pre-purchase.
• Anyone tired of overpaying because they don't speak "mechanic."

HOW IT WORKS

1. Add your vehicle (year, make, model, mileage).
2. Tell Hank what's wrong — in plain English. Type, voice, or attach a photo.
3. Answer a few targeted questions. Hank narrows it down like a real tech would.
4. Get a complete diagnosis: what's broken, how bad it is, what to buy, what it costs, and how to fix it.
5. Save it to your garage. Track parts ordered, work completed, and total spend per vehicle.

FEATURES

• AI-powered diagnostic chat with confidence scoring
• Severity ratings (Minor, Moderate, Major, Safety-Critical)
• Complete work orders with parts, costs, and step-by-step instructions
• Garage manager — unlimited vehicles, photos, and service history
• Diagnosis history with full transcripts
• Parts and supplies hub with OEM part numbers and search links
• Glossary that explains mechanic-speak in plain English
• Dark mode by default (because most garages aren't well-lit)

SUBSCRIPTION

Mobile Master Mechanic offers two paid tiers in addition to limited free use:

• Day Pass — 24-hour unlimited access for one-off jobs
• Monthly Pro — recurring monthly subscription with unlimited diagnostics, work orders, and garage history

Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription anytime from your Google Play account → Subscriptions.

IMPORTANT

Mobile Master Mechanic provides AI-generated guidance for informational purposes only. It is not a replacement for inspection or repair by a qualified mechanic, especially for safety-critical issues like brakes, steering, or fuel leaks. Always consult a licensed professional before performing repairs you are not trained to complete safely.

ABOUT US

Mobile Master Mechanic is built by Angie's Auto Supplies — a third-generation, woman-owned, dealer-run resource hub based in Oregon. We've spent decades watching honest people get talked into expensive repairs they didn't need. This app is our answer.
```

## Screenshot capture script (when you have an Android device or emulator)

The Play Store accepts up to 8 phone screenshots. Recommended set, in order:

1. **Home screen with Hank** — show Hank's avatar, the welcome state, the "Start a Diagnosis" CTA.
2. **Diagnostic chat in progress** — show a real conversation, Hank asking a follow-up question.
3. **Diagnosis result** — show the severity badge, confidence bar, work-order list.
4. **Work order detail** — show parts list with OEM numbers, estimated cost, step-by-step.
5. **Garage screen** — show vehicle list with photos.
6. **Vehicle detail with service history** — show the timeline.
7. **Supplies hub** — show parts categories.
8. **Subscription screen** — show pricing tiers.

You can capture these from a real device (Power + Volume Down on most Android phones) or from Android Studio's emulator (camera button in the side toolbar).
